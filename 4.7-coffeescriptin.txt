$(document).ready ->

To attach a click event handler to the displayWeaponForm div, you'd do this:
$('a#displayWeaponForm').click (event) ->
  # code goes here
The correct answer should be:
$(document).ready ->
  $('a#displayWeaponForm').click (event) ->
    $('div#newWeapon').show()
    $('a#displayWeaponForm').hide()
    event.preventDefault()
